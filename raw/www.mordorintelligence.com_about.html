OUR STORY

A Decade of Excellence

From a bold vision to an industry leader, our journey has been powered by ambition and innovation.

2014

Mordor Intelligence Becomes Reality

Self-started and bootstrapped, we officially opened for business. What had started out with a few people soon turned into a group of 20.

2015-2019

Building Our Expertise

We expand our abilities, widen our industry reach, and enjoy a 5x growth in customers, becoming a preferred partner for market intelligence.

2020

Adapting to Change

We switched to remote operations in March 2020, prioritizing employee well-being while ensuring uninterrupted service.

2021

Commitment to Quality

We earned ISO 9001:2015 certification, reaffirming our commitment to quality and reliable insights.

2022

Driving Social Impact

We launched our CSR program to equip underprivileged students with employable skills, delivering a 50x social ROI.

2023

Strategic Growth & Industry Credibility

  • Acquired a majority stake in Global Market Insights (GMI) Group.
  • Became a member of the Market Research Society of India (MRSI), reinforcing ethical research practices.

2024

Expanding Innovation & Presence

  • Launched Synapse, your market intelligence command centre.
  • Launched myRA AI™, your personal research assistant.
  • Joined ESOMAR for global research standards.
  • Represented Mordor Intelligence at FIND – Design Fair Asia and OSEA Singapore.

2025

Scaling New Heights

  • Achieved Great Place to Work® recognition for three consecutive years.
  • Grew our client base 10x, expanding global trust and reach.
  • Received ISO/IEC 27001:2022 certification for information security and risk management.

Get access to the right expertise at the right time. Our distributed model introduces you to subject matter experts across the globe, offering industry-specific, context-relevant, and timely insights.

Strategic Location

India-based, we deliver research at scale with world-class quality. This allows us to match cost with accuracy and help you achieve the highest ROI without compromising depth or credibility.
