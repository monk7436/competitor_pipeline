What we do?

Aakri, powered by ReSustainability is your go-to app for managing all types of household waste right from your doorstep. Whether you need to get rid of used diapers, sanitary waste, e-waste, scrap, or biomedical disposals, Aakri makes it simple, safe, and quick. Just open the app, schedule a pickup, and our trained team will take care of the rest. You can track your pickup in real time and even earn instant payments for recyclable items like paper, plastic, and electronics. No more storing waste or dealing with unreliable vendors. With Aakri, your waste is collected responsibly and sent to certified recycling or treatment centers. It’s designed to make your life easier and your home cleaner, while helping build a cleaner, zero-waste future for everyone.

Rebuilding Tomorrow Starts at Your Doorstep!

India produces over 3.4 million tonnes of plastic waste every year, according to official reports, while broader estimates place the real figure closer to 9.3 million tonnes. Only a fraction of this is ever recycled, and the rest ends up choking landfills, drains, and ecosystems. Aakri steps in to close this gap. More than just a waste collection app, Aakri is a solution built for homes, communities, and the planet. From doorstep pickups to certified recycling and responsible disposal, we make it easy for you to manage waste the right way. Every item you let go through Aakri fuels a cleaner home, a smarter city, and a more sustainable future for all.
