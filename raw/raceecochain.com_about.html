About RACE

We are RACE ECO CHAIN LTD.

Welcome To RACE ♻️

Revolutionizing waste management

Race Eco Chain Ltd, where RACE stands for Recycling And Circular Economy, is dedicated to waste management. As one of the fastest-growing supply chain for plastic waste in India, we are committed to organizing the unorganized waste industry with the following initiatives:

PAN India Network

A comprehensive network for Waste Suppliers ensuring efficient waste management across India.

Dedicated Regional Collection Centers

Specialized centers to streamline collection and recycling.

Promoting Circular Economy

Leading to reduced material hoarding risk, reduces market volatility, and improves profitability for all stakeholders in the value chain.⁠

Digitizing Supply Chain with RACE App

Enhancing efficiency and transparency through digital solutions.

ESG Focus

Prioritizing Environmental, Social, and Governance factors in our operations. As well as creating ESG complaint Supply chain for waste procurement.

Community Engagement

Hosted India’s first Talent recognition events for Waste Supplier code named ‘Udaan’ also actively promoting awareness and participation in waste management and recycling.

Washing Plants

Setting up washing plants across India focusing on B2B (Bottle to Bottle) manufacturing raw materials Supply.

Aditionally Race Eco Chain is also involved in the business of Biomass Briquettes and recycled products, investing in an idea whose time has come. Promoting a niche line of products made from recycled plastic of the highest quality, promoting sustainability and providing e2e traceability.

To join our mission, reach us at communications@raceecochain.com. We always look forward to collaborating!

We are RACE ECO CHAIN LTD.

Welcome To RACE ♻️

Revolutionizing waste management

We are RACE (Recycling and Circular Economy)- an environmentally conscious initiative with a mission to reduce the carbon footprint caused by plastic waste. Our complete vertical set-up is responsible for the collection and audit of the ‘waste’ to the end product using AI & Blockchain technology establishing networks to enable traceability.

RACE has built an Artificial Intelligence enabled app, using blockchain for secure transactions to connect buyers and sellers. Recycled pet fabric processed at our GRS (Global Recycle Standards) certified spinning and weaving mills come with the Traceable circle of trust, for the first time ever.

RACE Eco chain is involved in the aggregation of plastic waste and production of recycled products investing in an idea whose time has come. Promoting a niche line of products made from recycled plastic of the highest quality, promoting sustainability and providing e2e traceability.

Vision & Mission Statement

The ‘Waste-to-Energy’ and Waste Management market in India is set to be a $14bn opportunity by the year 2025. The population of 1.3 billion in India currently generates 62 million tons of municipal solid waste per year. And by 2027, India is set to become the world’s most populous country as per projections of the United Nations with 7 new megacities. At this exponential population and urban growth rate, landfills almost 90% of the size of Bengaluru would be required for dumping if the waste remains untreated. 

The scale of opportunity may be gauged by the fact that India has the potential to generate 3GW of electricity from waste by 2050. The benefits of effective waste management are immense. India presents an opportunity in numerous subsectors of waste management including municipal solid waste, electronic waste, bio-medical waste, agricultural waste, and others. It is predicted that India has the potential to generate 3GW of electricity from waste by 2050.

Team Race is building a robust supply chain process starting from collecting / procuring the waste, adding value through quality & efficiency, to providing it as a raw material to the recycling units and also consuming it ourselves by the ways of producing go to market products which are 100% recycled. Our business operations are led by the following four objectives:

In the last few years, RACE has established its foot hold in most states of India and has built a large team of 500+ suppliers. We are catering and selling to almost every sizeable recycling organization in India ensuring the top quarter of the list considers Race as a preferred partner in the scheme of things. Team Race aspires and look forward to grow the footprint with each passing day and become a PAN India organization cultivating and supporting the entire chain through developing and deploying Business Process Management, Best Practices, Technology and L&D for this sector. Our operations also include developing adequate and relevant infrastructure to infuse efficiency and productivity to the cycle ranging from strategic warehousing to logistics to advanced machinery and so forth.

RACE operations involve end to end supply chain cycle for empowering the processing of this waste through multiple tollgates and bring back the waste to life through the virtues of Reuse, Reduce and Recycle.

1

To identify and support the development of new technologies that can help create a cleaner and greener environment.

2

To boost and augment the entire waste supply chain by leveraging science, technology, and innovation.

3

To create circular economic models that are financially viable for waste management.

4

To streamline waste handling in India.
