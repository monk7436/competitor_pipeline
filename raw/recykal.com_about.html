Our Story

Our Thought

But how?

We started with a 2 year ground research, which we dub as MBA in Waste Management, with the main
campus of study being Landfills and Waste Generating entities. With a goal of digitising and bridging the
gaps between stakeholders, the team honed their skills and brought their vision to life.

What happened next?

Recykal was launched! In a matter of 2 years we scaled from 2 states to 30+ States and UTs Waste channeling went from 10,000 MT in a year to 200,000 MT in 2021 Recykal Marketplace, EPR Loop, Smart Centre solutions and Deposit Return System (DRS) Take back program took the
industry by storm!

Recykal has grown into one of Indias largest sustainability tech platforms operational across 30+ States and Union Territories, enabling the channelization of over 1.2 million metric tonnes of material, and helping mitigate 1.24 million tonnes of CO equivalent emissions.
