Waste
Management
Solutions

Zero Waste Program (ZWP)

Saahas Zero Waste's comprehensive Zero Waste Program (ZWP) offers end-to-end waste management in India based on circular economy principles. Services include waste audits, reduction, segregation, collection, infrastructure setup, processing, data analysis, tracking, and certification.

Extended Producer Responsibility (Plastic & E-Waste)

Saahas Zero Waste offers customized solutions to help businesses comply with Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR) regulations for plastic and e-waste. Our comprehensive approach includes CPCB registration, collection, aggregation of waste, ensuring material traceability, and handling annual return filing. We focus on maximum recovery through partnerships with authorized plastic and e-waste processors. Additionally, we emphasize on Social Inclusion, providing opportunities for all, and also specialize in Refurbishment (laptops) to extend the life cycle of electronics.

Recycled Products

Circle Up by Saahas Zero Waste, closes the loop by transforming discarded materials into valuable products while supporting marginalized communities. Through recycling and upcycling, we minimize the need for new resources and enhance environmental impact. Our products range from eco-boards (alternate to plywood) to stationery, furniture to upcycled textile products, ceramic to recycled paper .

Holistic Solutions
For Every Sector

Saahas Zero Waste provides professional waste management services across various sectors, ensuring compliance with waste regulations and driving sustainability goals through resource recovery. By leveraging cutting-edge technology and a deep understanding of all types of waste management, we offer customised solutions that address the specific needs of our clients.

Companies

Saahas Zero Waste helps brands comply with waste management regulations like the Solid Waste Management Rules (2016), Plastic Waste Management Rules, and Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR). The services range from processing of food waste, horticultural waste to resource recovery from dry waste and C&D waste.

Tech Parks

We provide waste management solutions for tech parks, including waste audits, segregation, food waste composting and bio-methanation, dry waste sorting, and reporting. This helps attract tenants and position tech parks as responsible enterprises.

Townships

Townships come under the ambit of Solid Waste Management Rules (2016) and need to comply to the waste management rules, Saahas Zero Waste (SZW) has played a significant role in the successful implementation of complete waste systems in townships. This includes introduction of segregation at source, collection of segregated waste, setting up an integrated waste management facility for food waste and dry waste.

Educational Institutions

Saahas Zero Waste (SZW) works with educational institutions to provide comprehensive services beginning with a waste audit, systems design, setting up and then operating the system for different wastes. Through robust processes and data driven reporting, we ensure that these institutions comply with the waste management rules.

Property Consultants & Facility Managers

Saahas Zero Waste (SZW) partners with leading names in the industry to provide a decade of essential services in waste management. These services align with resource recovery and a circular economy which in turn helps builders and tenants report their ESG and Sustainability goals.

Producer, Importers and Brand Owners

We offer services to PIBOs including registration, reverse logistics, traceability, reporting, and partnerships with authorized recyclers, this helps them meet EPR regulations and gain EPR credits.

Residential Communities

We operate efficient waste management systems for residences, including segregation, collection, processing, sorting, and tracking, ensuring authorized end destinations for dry waste.

Consulting Organizations

With extensive on-ground experience, Saahas Zero Waste provides consulting to Urban Local Bodies, Panchayats, Rural departments, national and international agencies, setting up infrastructure like Materials Recovery Facilities (MRFs) and bio-CNG systems, and advising on EPR compliance and social inclusion.
