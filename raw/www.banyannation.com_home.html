Circular Economy Advancing Sustainability in Plastics

The circular economy is a promising solution to the plastic waste crisis. Through the prioritisation of the reuse, repair and recycling of plastic materials, we can minimise waste and conserve resources. A key aspect to recycling and recovering plastic waste into valuable products like plastic granules that can be used in the packaging industry. Plastic granules are a fundamental building block of many plastic items and can be derived from recycled plastic waste through a meticulous process. Through the transformation of discarded plastic waste into high – quality plastic granules we can create a sustainable cycle. For plastic production from recycled plastic granules, there is a reduction in the demand for virgin plastic conservation of energy and minimization of greenhouse gas emissions. In order to further strengthen the circular economy for plastics, it is crucial to invest in advanced recycling technologies that convert waste to plastic granules. These technologies can efficiently break down complex waste into components, hence enabling the production of high-quality plastic granules.

FAQS

What are the main processes Banyan Nation uses for recycling ?

There are standardised processes followed by most plastic recycling companies in india. The main process followed by Banyan Nation is as follows : It first goes inward to the storage warehouse then to PSS (Pre-sorting station) operation then to multiple stages of the washing line and then to the conversion to flakes (washed). This is followed by color sorting to the silo bank ten to the batch mixer. From here it goes to the extrusion process, followed by the bagging unit where the pellets are stored. The final stage leads to the finished goods storage.

What types of plastics do you recycle?

Banyan Nation is currently focusing on rigid packaging with the primary focus on recycling polyethylene (PE) and polypropylene (PP) plastics.

What products are made from Banyan Nations recycled plastics ?

Products like bottles used for packaging application for hair care, personal care, home care, fabric care, chemicals, and lubricant oils by FMCG and Lube brands are made from Banyan Nations recycled plastics. The following are also made – premium blow moulded rHDPE packaging, Premium rHDPE for tubes and Premium rHDPE for flexible packaging. Along with this also premium blow-moulded rPP packaging, premium rPP closures and Injection-moulded articles.

Can businesses partner with Banyan Nation?

Yes, businesses can collaborate with Banyan Nation. A major form of collaboration is through the selling of plastic scrap, purchasing of recycled plastic granules, and buying of EPR credits.
